Transforming How India Hires Construction Workers

India has over 60 million construction workers — yet hiring them remains broken, informal, and deeply inefficient. Shramico changes that by connecting employers with verified, skilled workers through a seamless mobile-first platform.

Currently live in Maharashtra (Mumbai & Pune), Shramico handles verification, matching, and communication — so both sides can focus on what matters: the work.
