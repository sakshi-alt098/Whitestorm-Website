Our Journey

From Zero to Storm

Every great company has a beginning. Here's ours.

2024 — Q1

Whitestorm Is Founded

Swapnil Rathod and Balaji Chauhan come together with a shared vision: to build a venture studio that creates tech companies solving India's biggest workforce challenges. Whitestorm is incorporated in Mumbai.

🌪️ The Beginning
2024 — Q2

Research & Validation Phase

The team spends months doing deep field research — interviewing construction workers, contractors, and employers across Maharashtra. The problem is clear: hiring skilled blue-collar workers is broken.

🔍 Research
2024 — Q3

Shramico Launches

India's first dedicated construction worker hiring platform goes live. Shramico connects employers and skilled workers through a seamless mobile-first experience. The response is immediate and overwhelming.

🚀 First Launch
2025 — Now

Growing the Portfolio

Whitestorm is actively building its next ventures. The studio is expanding its team, deepening its research into new markets, and preparing to launch more category-defining products.
