Our Journey

From Zero to Storm

Every great company has a beginning. Here's ours.

2024 — Q1

Whitestorm Is Founded

Swapnil Rathod and Balaji Chauhan come together with a shared vision: to build a technology company that creates products solving India's biggest workforce challenges. Whitestormm is incorporated in Mumbai.

🌪️ The Beginning
2024 — Q2

Research & Validation Phase

The team spends months doing deep field research — interviewing construction workers, contractors, and employers across Maharashtra. The problem is clear: hiring skilled blue-collar workers is broken.

🔍 Research
2024 — Q3

Shramico Launches

India's first dedicated construction worker hiring platform goes live. Shramico connects employers and skilled workers through a seamless mobile-first experience. The response is immediate and overwhelming.

🚀 First Launch
2025 — Now

Scaling Shramico

Whitestormm is focused on scaling Shramico across India. The team is expanding, deepening its presence in new cities, and continuing to build the future of construction workforce hiring.
