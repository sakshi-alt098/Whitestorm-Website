# Scope: Hero & Landing Page Integration

## Objectives
Harmonize the 3D `LabEnvironment` (hero) and `LandingPage` components under the new light theme, maintaining all animations and interactions.

- **Background Assets**: Ensure `LabEnvironment` utilizes `light-lab-bg.png` (or equivalent light styling) instead of the dark background image.

- **Navigation (Navbar)**: Ensure the `Navbar` links and logo are readable both before and after scrolling (class `nav-scrolled`).
- **Interactive Check**: Verify that 3D physics, carousel drag gestures, and scroll fade-ins are intact and function smoothly.
