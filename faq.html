About Whitestormm

4 questions
What is Whitestormm?

Whitestormm is a technology company focused on solving India's biggest workforce challenges. We identify high-impact problems, validate them through deep research, and build technology-driven products to solve them at scale.

Our first product, Shramico, is India's dedicated platform for hiring skilled construction workers — connecting employers with verified workers through a seamless mobile-first experience.

Who are the founders of Whitestormm?

Whitestormm was founded by Swapnil Rathod (Founder & CEO) and Balaji Chauhan (Co-Founder & COO). Together they bring complementary skills — product vision and operational excellence — to build products that matter.

You can learn more about them on our About page.

How does Whitestormm choose what to build?

We follow a rigorous 3-step process: (1) Identify — We look for large, underserved markets with clear pain points, especially in India's blue-collar and gig economy sectors. (2) Validate — We spend months doing field research, interviewing real users before writing a single line of code. (3) Build — Only after strong validation do we commit to building a full product.

We believe great startups are born from deep empathy with the problem, not from trends or investor hype.

Where is Whitestorm based?

Whitestormm is headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. While we're India-first in our approach, our vision is to build products with global potential.

About Shramico

4 questions
What is Shramico?

Shramico is Whitestormm's flagship product — India's dedicated platform for hiring skilled construction workers. It connects employers (contractors, builders, developers) with verified, skilled workers through a seamless mobile-first experience.

Visit shramico.com to learn more or get started.

How does Shramico work?

For Employers: Post a job requirement, browse verified worker profiles, and hire the right person for your site — in minutes, not days.

For Workers: Create a profile showcasing your skills and experience, discover job opportunities nearby, and get hired directly by trusted employers.

Shramico handles verification, matching, and communication so both sides can focus on what matters — the work.

Is Shramico available in my city?

Shramico is currently focused on Maharashtra, with Mumbai and Pune as the primary markets. We are actively expanding to other major construction hubs across India.

Check shramico.com for the latest coverage updates or reach out to us at contact us for availability in your region.

How do I join Shramico as a worker or employer?

Simply visit shramico.com and sign up. The onboarding process is designed to be quick and mobile-friendly — you can be up and running in under 5 minutes.

Workers will need to complete a basic profile and skills verification. Employers can start posting requirements immediately after registration.
